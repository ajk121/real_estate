Sales

In many cases a property sale or acquisition will constitute the largest investment an individual may undertake. We therefore fully understand the importance of managing this process to ensure our clients’ interests are best served, either in terms of the sale of an existing property or the sourcing and purchase of a new one. We can advise you of any up and coming opportunities or those we may have on our books. So whether you are looking to sell or buy a property in Dubai we are well placed to ensure you get the best out of your investment.

Letting

Blackstone Real Estate manages a wide range of properties across Dubai on behalf of overseas and local owners. We provide an efficient lettings and property management service to a our clients, from individual property owners to investors with large portfolios. Blackstone Real Estate offers a comprehensive and professional one-stop solution for all your property management requirements. Our service includes sourcing and vetting suitable tenants, managing payments, regular updates and statements for landlords, quarterly property inspections, full check-in and check-out inventory reviews. Properties are professionally cleaned and painted after each tenancy as well as maintained on an ongoing basis.
